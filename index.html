Welcome To Trash Run!

What's Our Project?

Our game that we created is titled Trash Run and it is based off of a enduring issue of environment pollution. The point of our game is to advocate for stronger and greener communities, we hope playing our game is able to teach and motivate the player to make a positive impact on their environment. Through playing our game the audience is able to gain an understanding of how crucial it is to keep the envrionment clear, while still being entertained and
